About Us

Global Parts Enterprises(L.L.C) is a professionally managed, multimillion dollar organization, catering to a global market in export and re-export of spare parts to over 100 countries Worldwide (Genuine and Replacement). Global Parts Enterprises L.L.C, came into existence through the vision of a first generation enterpreneur.

In a short span of time, it has built an enviable clientele and reputation of trustworthiness. Handling over 100 genuine and 500 replacement brands, the organization exports to over 100 countries worldwide, with a formidable presence in the USA.
