About Company

Global Parts Enterprises(L.L.C) is a professionally managed, multimillion dollar organization, catering to a global market in export and re-export of spare parts to over 100 countries Worldwide (Genuine and Replacement).

Global Parts Enterprises L.L.C, came into existence through the vision of a first generation enterpreneur.

In a short span of time, it has built an enviable clientele and reputation of trustworthiness. Handling over 100 genuine and 500 replacement brands, the organization exports to over 100 countries worldwide, with a formidable presence in the USA.

One of our many strengths is a combined inventory of over 10 million line items which enables (JIT) Just in Time delivery. Therefore, it gives the organization an edge in competing efficiently and also pass on the benefits of cost advantage to our customers.

Our Mission

"To be the best known Trading Organization offering excellent service and have a global presence by gaining the trust and complete satisfaction of our Clientele"

Our Vision

Not just being a Trading Organization, but building relationships based on "Integrity with Performance“ and single-minded focus to achieve business objectives.

Our Strategy

Cost advantage-Giving extra to the customer by way of quality but at a better price than competitors Differentiation-High perceived value from stakeholders to deliver customer focus and professionalism.
